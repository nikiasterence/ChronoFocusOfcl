Trigonometry Formulas

Sine Ratio

sin θ = opposite/hypotenuse

Ratio of the length of the opposite side to the length of the hypotenuse

Cosine Ratio

cos θ = adjacent/hypotenuse

Ratio of the length of the adjacent side to the length of the hypotenuse

Tangent Ratio

tan θ = opposite/adjacent

Ratio of the length of the opposite side to the length of the adjacent side

Cosecant

csc θ = 1/sin θ

Reciprocal of the sine function

Secant

sec θ = 1/cos θ

Reciprocal of the cosine function

Cotangent

cot θ = 1/tan θ

Reciprocal of the tangent function

Pythagorean Identity 1

sin²θ + cos²θ = 1

Fundamental Pythagorean identity

Pythagorean Identity 2

1 + tan²θ = sec²θ

Pythagorean identity involving tangent and secant

Pythagorean Identity 3

1 + cot²θ = csc²θ

Co-function Identity 2

Co-function Identity 3

tan(90° - θ) = cot θ

Relationship between tangent and cotangent of complementary angles

Even-Odd Identity 1

sin(-θ) = -sin θ

Even-Odd Identity 2

cos(-θ) = cos θ

Even-Odd Identity 3

Sine Addition Formula

Formula for the sine of the sum of two angles

Cosine Addition Formula

cos(A+B) = cosA cosB - sinA sinB

Formula for the cosine of the sum of two angles

Tangent Addition Formula

tan(A+B) = (tanA + tanB)/(1 - tanA tanB)

Half Angle Tangent

Product-to-Sum 1

Product-to-Sum 2

Product-to-Sum 3

Converting product of sine and cosine to sum of sines
